Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hoplist?
Hoplist is a community that curates’ unique activities that are designed and hosted by inspiring people in your city who are passionate about diverse experiences and want to share their skills, hobbies or interests with those around them. These go beyond typical tours or classes, it's an opportunity for anyone to share their hobbies, skills, or expertise with the people around, and for you to immerse yourself in your host’s unique world. Hoplist aims to give life a whole new dimension by bringing the world together through shared interests and experiences.
How does Hoplist work?
Hoplist connects people together through shared interest, hobbies or skills - allowing people to transform their passion into what we call an “experience” for people to engage in. It's easy to get started with it, all you need is to become a host and create the activity you want to share or find an activity you want to be a part of. We make use of Geolocation API to only curate hosted experiences for guests around you and the people following your profile, making it easier to find your tribe and make interesting connections.
What can I do on Hoplist?
You can do it all! Discover things to do, create and host your activity, share real moments, meet and connect with people, do more of what you love, share your interests, try something new. All we aim to do is bring people together through unique, interesting and personalized experience.
Where can I use Hoplist?
Hoplist can be used anywhere and everywhere! The app is constantly updated with your live location to help you discover things to do around you. Also, you can set a custom range of location you'd like to receive activities from. There's always a community for you!
How much does it cost to use Hoplist?
Hoplist is absolutely free to use. They are no charges whatsoever. However, if you are hosting a paid experience, we charge a 5% commission fee.
If I host a paid experience, when do I get my payout?
We release your payment the day after you host an activity. Although different financial institutions have different resolve dates which determine if your payout can be released on weekends or public holidays.
What does it mean to be verified on Hoplist?
Identification is required for verification before an experience can be created, it's our way of maintaining security and trust in the community while also fighting against fraud. We don't permit nor encourage users under the age of 18 to join Hoplist, but they can join by sending us a parental approval letter.
